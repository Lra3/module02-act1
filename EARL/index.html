Axie Infinity

PC/Mobile Game


Written by: Earl Nakpil on November 9, 2021


Axie Infinity is a NFT-based online video game developed by Vietnamese studio Sky Mavis, which uses Ethereum-based cryptocurrency AXS and SLP. As of May 2021, it is the most expensive NFT collection.

Initial release date: March 2018
Developers: Sky Mavis, SKY Mavis PTE. Ltd.
Publisher: Sky Mavis
Genres: Non-fungible token, Online game, Strategy Video Game
